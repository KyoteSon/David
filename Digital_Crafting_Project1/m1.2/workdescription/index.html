The Work Description

While looking at the things I'm going to redesign at my house, I found an object that I always felt uncomfortable using.

As soon as we come out of the shower, we should remove the moisture with a towel. Otherwise, we can catch a cold.

So most toilets have towels next to shower facilities. There is a towel rack right next to the shower booth at my house, and I felt a lot of inconvenience using it.

The reason is that I need enough space to hang a towel, but the towel rack in my house is designed very small.

So not only is it hard to hang a towel, but even if I hang a towel hardly, it falls easily. So I'm going to design this towel rack as a more practical towel rack.

The design draft I'm currently thinking of is making a towel rack that combines both practicality and design. Since the wall with the towel rack attached to my bathroom is narrow, I will use this space to make a practical towel rack. It will be designed so that clothes or shower gowns can be hung, not just one towel hanger
