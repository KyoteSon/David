The Work Description

I learned Rhino for the first time in the Digital Crafting Basic class I took last winter semester.
This ring was the first work I've ever made with Rhino. At first, it was a ring inspired by the Korean and American flags.
For this project, I wanted to make my own ring. To that end, I made my logo and designed it on the ring.
It was difficult to try casting for the first time, but it was a really fun and interesting experience.
